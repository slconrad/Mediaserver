Access Galaxy Media Server

Welcome to Galaxy Media Server, your personal entertainment destination. To begin streaming our curated collection of timeless classics and popular television series, you'll need to set up your access through Plex.

Getting Started

Sign up or login at Plex.tv to access this exclusive server. Please note: Galaxy Media Server is invite-only, ensuring a premium experience for our curated community of entertainment enthusiasts.

What You'll Enjoy

  • Classic Cinema: Explore our extensive collection of timeless films spanning multiple decades and genres—from iconic dramas and adventures to beloved comedies that have stood the test of time.
  • Popular Television: Binge complete series including legendary shows and contemporary favorites. Discover golden-age classics alongside modern hits, all available at your fingertips.
  • Media Requests: Can't find what you're looking for? Simply submit a media request, and our team will work to add it to the server. Your entertainment preferences shape our growing library.

Privacy & Control

As an invite-only private server, Galaxy Media Server prioritizes your privacy and viewing experience. Stream anywhere, anytime, with complete control over your personal library and preferences.

If you don't have an invitation code yet, please reach out to your Galaxy Media Server administrator for access.
